Purdys Chocolatier Website

VIEW WEBSITE

Overview

Purdys.com is the main website for Purdy's Chocolatier online business to provide customers in Canada and the United States fantastic chocolates made in Vancouver, BC. The website is powered by ASP.NET and SQL with front-end HTML, CSS, and Javascript support.

I played a role in designing the look and feel of the website using front-end web coding. as well as developed certain functionalities to improve user experience using C# and .NET.

What I Use

HTML5, CSS3, JAVASCRIPT + JQUERY, ASP.NET, C#

My Duties

  • Collaborated in redesigning the theme of the website based off of Creative Department's new brand reconstruction.
  • Improved core features of the website in order to provide robust user experience for customers.
  • Communicated closely with Web Sales Department and Director to provide support on bug fixes and promotional updates.
  • Influenced modern web design and development strategies to enhance SEO.

My Impact

Purdys Chocolatier website, with its new branding and simplified flow of the site, has increased their sales bi-weekly from 30-40% increases over the same weeks in previous years. This increase is also impacted through the mobile version of the site.
