Overview

Purdys Chocolatier Group Purchase and Fundraising Program is a seasonal promotional program that Purdys offer to Canada for customers to buy fanastic chocolates in bulk with a discount of 25% off for Group sales or 25% redeemable as profit for fundraising campaigns.

Purdys Group Purchase and Fundraising Website is built using a free template called nopCommerce, which is modified using ASP.NET and C# with front-end HTML, CSS and Javascript coding for visual design.

What I Use

HTML5, CSS3, JAVASCRIPT + JQUERY, ASP.NET, C#, MYSQL

My Duties

  • Collaborated with Creative Department to share ideas and to implement front-end seasonal themes.
  • Developed major tools and functions based off user study to better serve customers and office support teams.
  • Managed user test groups to study demographics, gather feedback and information to improve customer experience and satasfaction.
  • Acted as the primary liason between the departments who are involved in the project.
  • Volunteered to be the main technical support during peak seasons to provide telemarketers and customers across Canada with troubleshooting.

My Impact

The result of my impact provided fantastic feedback from customers, new and returning, on the usability of the website. The customers liked the simplicity and the "to the point" understanding of the design. Through constant communication from the departments we were able remain vigilant on resolving issue with speed and efficiency. In 2013, we resulted in a growth of 40% over 2012
