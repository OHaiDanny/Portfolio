Inventory Mapping Software

VISUAL STUDIO, C#, MYSQL

Overview

To prepare for the fiscal year inventory count, our team was tasked to make sure that the mapping of products in our storage matches the records we have on a database software called Syteline.

Duties

  • Developed from scratch a robust mapping tool using C# for fiscal inventory counts.
  • Designed and maintained SQL database of inventory SKUS to provide one-to-one, or one-to-many mapping.
  • Demonstrated excellent communication with team members and departments to satasfiy user needs.

Outcome

The successful construction of this automatic mapping software reduced the workload during the fiscal years which allowed the company to perform rapid inventory sweeps.
